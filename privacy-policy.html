PocketLedger does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information or financial records with the developer or third parties.

PocketLedger provides encrypted backup, restore, and CSV export features. These operations are initiated by the user, and the resulting files are stored in locations selected or controlled by the user.

PocketLedger does not use advertising services, analytics services, account services, or third-party tracking technologies.

App data remains on the user's device until the user deletes the data, clears the app's storage, or uninstalls the application.

Backup and exported files remain in the locations selected by the user until the user deletes them.

PocketLedger uses local storage and encrypted backup functionality to help protect user data. However, no storage or security method can guarantee absolute protection.

PocketLedger is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
